Reimagining the iPhone

What if your phone could do more, without doing too much? Meet iPhone Fold: the first iPhone that bends the rules of design. A seamless blend of innovation and elegance, built to transform the way you see, work, and create.

Design That Adapts to You

Crafted from aerospace-grade titanium and shielded by next-generation Ceramic Glass, iPhone Fold opens into a stunning 7.9-inch Dynamic Retina XDR display. When folded, it fits perfectly in your palm; compact, durable, and impossibly sleek.

Power Beyond Limits

At the heart of iPhone Fold is the A19 Pro Fusion Chip, designed for unmatched speed and efficiency. From multitasking to AI-assisted creativity, every gesture feels instant, every idea effortless.

FlexMode Camera. A New Angle on Photography.

Capture, create, and share with freedom. With FlexMode, you can set your iPhone Fold at any angle, record hands-free, take group photos with ease, or film cinematic shots from perspectives you never thought possible.

A Hinge Designed Like a Work of Art

At the center of iPhone Fold is a precision-engineered hinge, crafted with microscopic accuracy. It opens smoothly, holds its position effortlessly, and disappears when you need it most—so all you notice is the experience.

Sound That Expands With the Screen.

Spatial Audio adapts dynamically as you unfold the display. Whether folded or fully open, sound feels wider, richer, and perfectly balanced—immersing you without overwhelming you.

iPhone Fold combines portability with power, unfolding into a stunning, immersive display whenever you need more space. Designed for flexibility, built for everyday life — and crafted with the elegance you expect from Apple.
